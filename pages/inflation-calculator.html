What is the average inflation rate in India?▾
India's average CPI inflation over the past decade has been 5.5–6.5%. The RBI targets 4% inflation (with a 2% band). For long-term planning, using 6% is a reasonable conservative assumption.
How does inflation affect FIRE planning?▾
Inflation is the single biggest risk to FIRE plans. At 6% inflation, your purchasing power halves every 12 years. A ₹50,000/month expense today costs ₹1.60 lakh/month in 20 years — so your post-retirement corpus must generate returns above inflation.
