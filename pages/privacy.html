Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What we collect

MiddleClassMoney.in is a free financial tools website. All calculations run entirely in your browser — we never see, transmit, or store the financial numbers you enter. When you create a free account to download PDF reports, we collect your name and email address only.

2. How we use your data

Your email is used only to: (a) authenticate your account for PDF downloads, and (b) send occasional updates about new tools and articles — you can unsubscribe any time. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to any third party.

3. Cookies and analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate usage (which pages are visited, traffic sources). This uses cookies. You can opt out of GA4 by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

4. Google AdSense

MiddleClassMoney.in displays advertisements via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other sites. You can opt out at google.com/settings/ads.

5. Affiliate links

Some links on this site are affiliate links to brokers such as Zerodha, Groww, and Upstox. If you click these and open an account, we may earn a referral commission at no extra cost to you. We only recommend services we consider legitimate and useful.

6. Data security

Account passwords are hashed before storage. Our servers use HTTPS encryption. We do not store any financial data you enter into calculators.
