SIP vs lumpsum — which is better?▾
SIP is better when you're investing regularly from salary. Lumpsum is better when you have a large amount (bonus, inheritance, property sale) and want to deploy it immediately. Historically in India, lumpsum in a market dip outperforms SIP over 10+ year horizons.
